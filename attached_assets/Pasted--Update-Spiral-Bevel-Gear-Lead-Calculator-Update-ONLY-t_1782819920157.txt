# Update Spiral Bevel Gear Lead Calculator

Update ONLY the Spiral Bevel Gear Lead Calculator in my existing Industrial Gear Engineering Calculator application. Do not modify any other modules or calculations.

## Objective

Simplify the lead calculator by removing unnecessary inputs and calculate the Equivalent Machining Lead using only Module, Number of Teeth, and Spiral Angle.

## Remove These Input Fields

Completely remove the following inputs from the Spiral Bevel Gear Lead Calculator:

- Pitch Cone Angle
- Face Width
- Face Width Ratio
- Mean Cone Distance
- Mean Pitch Diameter
- Any other input used only for lead calculation

These values must not be required for the lead calculation.

## Required Inputs

The calculator should require only:

1. Module (mm)
2. Number of Teeth
3. Spiral Angle (degrees)

## Calculation Method

Step 1:

Pitch Diameter (D)

D = Module × Number of Teeth

Step 2:

Equivalent Machining Lead (mm)

Lead = (π × D) ÷ tan(Spiral Angle)

where

D = Pitch Diameter (mm)

Spiral Angle = Degrees

Step 3:

Convert to Inches

Lead (inch) = Lead (mm) ÷ 25.4

## Display Results

Display the following:

• Module
• Number of Teeth
• Spiral Angle
• Pitch Diameter (mm)
• Equivalent Machining Lead (mm)
• Equivalent Machining Lead (inch)

## Show Complete Calculation

Display all calculation steps exactly like an engineering handbook.

Example:

Input

Module = 3 mm

Number of Teeth = 15

Spiral Angle = 35°

Step 1

Pitch Diameter

D = Module × Teeth

D = 3 × 15

D = 45 mm

Step 2

Equivalent Lead

Lead = (π × 45) ÷ tan(35°)

Lead = (3.141592654 × 45) ÷ 0.700207538

Lead = 201.90 mm

Step 3

Lead in Inches

Lead = 201.90 ÷ 25.4

Lead = 7.95 inch

## Validation

- Module must be greater than 0.
- Number of Teeth must be greater than 0.
- Spiral Angle must be between 1° and 89°.
- Display meaningful validation messages for invalid input.
